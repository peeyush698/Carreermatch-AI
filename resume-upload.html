💡 Resume Writing Tips

📋

Use PDF Format

Always submit your resume as PDF to preserve formatting across all ATS systems and devices.

🔍

Mirror Job Keywords

Copy exact keywords from the job description into your resume to pass ATS keyword filters.

📈

Quantify Everything

Add numbers to your achievements: "Improved performance by 30%" beats "Improved performance".

📄

One Page Rule

For freshers and students, keep your resume to one page. Recruiters spend 6 seconds on first scan.

⚡

Strong Action Verbs

Start every bullet with power verbs: Developed, Built, Optimized, Implemented, Designed, Led.

🎯

Tailor for Each Job

Customize your resume for each application. A tailored resume gets 3x more interview calls.
